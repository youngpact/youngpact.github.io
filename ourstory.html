Our story

Who are we?

We are two youngsters from Helsinki Finland passionate about changing the education for the better. Not all youngsters are brave enough to share their story, but we want to offer them the chance to do so at a low threshold. We are prepared to do everything we can to give youngsters a voice, share knowledge and skills and truly have an impact.

We got this idea, when we realised that there were no platforms or opportunities for us to tell our story. We wanted to tell our opinions and solutions, but there were simply no opportunities for us to do so.

When we were in lower secondary, we wanted to have an impact on the way people thought about their daily life. We wanted to be a part of the discussions. Well, we realised that if we had nowhere to turn, we needed to make a place where youngsters could go and tell their story.

We are now enthusiastic to offer these possibilities to other youngsters that want to tell their story, and are ready to help.

What we want, is for youth to have a voice. We are now just starting out, but will be active to create opportunities for youngsters.

We are now in high school. We know what the young want since we are young ourselves. We are currently on second grade of high school, and will graduate in 2021.
