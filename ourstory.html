Our story

Who are we?

We are a team of youngsters from Helsinki Finland passionate about providing opportunities and helping others. Not all youngsters are brave enough to take action, but we want to offer them the chance at a low threshold. We are prepared to do everything we can to help youngsters.

When we were in lower secondary, we wanted to have an impact on the way people thought about their daily life. We wanted to be a part of the discussions. Well, we realised that we had nowhere to turn, we needed to make a place where youngsters could go and tell their story. So many youngsters have amazing ideas and messages to tell, but they are hardly ever give the chance. Youngsters are such a big part of our society it would be a waste not to listen to them when making decisions. That is how the idea behind Youngpact was born.

We are now enthusiastic to offer these possibilities to other youngsters that want to tell their story, and are ready to help.

What we want, is for the youth to have a voice.
