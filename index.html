About

Our mission

We want to give the youth a chance to tell their own story and we want them to be their own teachers and impacters, but for that they need help. We can connect these youngsters to for example schools and events and they can go there to share their stories and solutions. We also organize lectures with professionals helping youngsters impact even better.

And what is a story? Stories can be what we have been through, what we feel, or what solutions we have. Stories are what the youngsters feel and what they have for a solution. Stories can also be examples of what not to do so they can be lessons. Stories are what we make them to be.

What do we use stories for? We can use these lessons and stories to better ourselves and our surroundings. We can learn a great deal from youngsters and their stories, since they have opinions to. They also have solutions and stories about their experiences.

What we need, is for youngsters to take their place in our discussions and have them tell their own points of views. We need these opinions to better the world. Young people can have solutions and ideas too. We need to give a voice to them and like this we can change the world one story at a time.

For a voice we need speakers, and any enthusiastic youngster can join our community. Don’t be intimidated to have a voice. Be brave to have an impact. Don’t misunderstand we ask a lot, but you can’t make an impact with something small.

What do we offer?

Networks


As a massive system that provides relationships with companies, events and other people that are interested in telling their own story.

Possibilities


To change the world. Learning new information about your fellow youngsters life and like this learning about your own. A chance to learn public speaking and reaching the world with your story and message.

Opportunities


To tell your own story and a possibility in that way to change the way your audience thinks.
