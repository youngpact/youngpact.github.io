About

Our mission

We at Youngpact believe that the youth are not offered enough opportunities to express themselves. Many youngsters have excellent ideas and solutions to today’s problems, but the opportunities they have to express these ideas are very limited. Today’s youngsters are tomorrow’s leaders. Therefore we believe that they should be a part of building a better tomorrow, and a world they will one day lead.

This can all be achieved through public speaking. Public speaking is a skill, that is extremely important, because it allows people to express themselves in a convincing way and deliver their message clearly. It is also a vital skill in working life, because most of the jobs today require some type of selling or convincing, and this can be learnt through public speaking. Public speaking is also a skill that is appreciated by employers, therefore giving you an advantage when applying for a job.

Our mission at Youngpact is to provide the youth with opportunities to learn about public speaking and organize events where the youth get to express their thoughts to the world and meet various companies and people this way connecting the leaders of tomorrow with the leaders of today Through Youngpact youngsters can learn vital skills, express their ideas and thoughts about their surroundings and build important networks. For companies Youngpact provides a unique channel for reaching youngsters as an audience and an opportunity to meet and even recruit capable and motivated youngsters.

What do we offer?

Networks


As a system that provides relationships with companies, events, professionals and other people that are interested in telling their own story.

Possibilities


To change the world. Learn new information from your fellow youngsters. And a chance to learn public speaking and reach the world with your story and message.

Opportunities


To tell your own story and a possibility in that way to change the way your audience thinks.
